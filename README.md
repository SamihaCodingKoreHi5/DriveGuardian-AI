# 🚗 DriveGuardian-AI

AI-powered vehicle health monitoring and predictive maintenance system that analyzes vehicle conditions and provides maintenance recommendations before critical failures occur.

---

## 🎯 Overview

DriveGuardian-AI helps vehicle owners and automotive enthusiasts assess the health of a vehicle using key indicators such as:

- Engine Temperature
- Brake Wear
- Battery Health
- Tire Health
- Vehicle Mileage

Based on these inputs, the system calculates an overall vehicle health score, determines risk levels, and generates maintenance recommendations.

## 🔧 How It Works

The system evaluates vehicle health based on predefined maintenance rules:

- High engine temperature reduces health score
- Excessive brake wear increases risk level
- Low battery health triggers maintenance alerts
- Poor tire condition impacts safety score
- High mileage contributes to long-term wear

The final score determines the overall risk category:

| Score  | Risk Level |
| ------ | ---------- |
| 80–100 | Low        |
| 60–79  | Medium     |
| 0–59   | High       |
